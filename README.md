# CertifyCX — ISO Certification Management Platform

**CertifyCX** is a comprehensive, end-to-end digital platform that simplifies the entire ISO certification journey — from the very first inquiry to the issuance of a globally recognized certificate. Built to serve businesses, auditors, certification bodies, and administrators, CertifyCX brings the traditionally slow and paper-heavy certification process into one streamlined, secure, and fully digital experience.

---

## What CertifyCX Does

CertifyCX acts as a single digital workspace where:

- **Companies** can apply for ISO certification, track their progress, upload compliance documents, make secure online payments, and receive their certificate — all from one dashboard.
- **Auditors** can manage assigned audits, review documentation, and submit their audit findings electronically.
- **Certification Bodies** can manage assigned applications, review audit reports, and issue final certification decisions.
- **Administrators** oversee the entire platform, manage users, assign auditors and certification bodies, track applications across regions, and maintain the global registry of accreditation and certification organizations.

---

## Key Platform Features

### 1. Role-Based Access Control
The platform operates on a five-role system, each with tailored dashboards, permissions, and workflows:

- **Super Admin** — full control over the entire platform, including users, regions, registries, pricing, and settings.
- **Regional Admin** — manages applications, users, and certification bodies within their assigned geographic region.
- **Auditor** — handles audit assignments, document review, and audit submissions.
- **Certification Body** — reviews audited applications and makes the final certification decision.
- **Client (Company)** — applies for certification, uploads documents, makes payments, and tracks their journey.

### 2. Complete Certification Workflow
The platform supports the full certification lifecycle:

- **Free Gap Analysis Quiz** — helps potential clients assess their readiness before applying.
- **Smart Application Form** — captures company details, scope, employee count, and industry.
- **AI-Based ISO Recommendation** — suggests the most suitable ISO standard based on the company's business activity.
- **Online Payment Integration** — secure Stripe-powered checkout with full and installment plan options.
- **Document Management** — clients upload required compliance documents directly to the platform.
- **Auditor Assignment & Review** — admins assign auditors; auditors review documents and submit their findings.
- **Certification Body Decision** — assigned CBs issue the final approval or rejection.
- **Digital Certificate Issuance** — successful applicants receive their IAF-recognized certificate.

### 3. Global Accreditation & Certification Body Registry
A fully managed, relational registry containing:

- **78 Accreditation Bodies** pre-loaded and ready to use.
- **Complete IAF country list (~240 countries)** for global coverage.
- **Certification Body profiles** — each CB can be linked to multiple Accreditation Bodies, countries covered, ISO standards offered, IAF sector codes, and pricing tiers (initial certification, surveillance, and recertification).
- **39 IAF Sector Codes** to classify scope of certification.
- **10 major ISO standards** supported: ISO 9001, 14001, 27001, 45001, 22000, 13485, 50001, 37001, 20000-1, 22301.

### 4. Payment-Gated Client Visibility
Certification Body details are only revealed to clients **after payment is completed**, protecting business confidentiality and ensuring a clean client experience until commitment is secured.

### 5. Multi-Region Operations
Operations are segmented across **Asia, Africa, Europe, North America, and South America**, each managed by its own Regional Admin, with region-based filtering built into every administrative view.

### 6. Full Multi-Language Support
The entire platform — including dashboards, forms, buttons, error messages, and emails — is available in:

- **English**
- **Spanish**
- **Arabic** (with full Right-to-Left layout support)

An automatic language suggestion system detects the user's browser language and prompts them to switch if a better-supported language is available.

### 7. Sara — Built-in AI Assistant
**Sara**, the platform's AI-powered ISO certification advisor, is available on the landing page and throughout the dashboard to:

- Answer questions about ISO standards and the certification process.
- Guide prospective clients through next steps.
- Capture leads by collecting interest and contact details from visitors.
- Provide instant, conversational support without needing a human agent.

### 8. Referral & Commission System
A built-in referral program where:

- Existing users can generate unique referral links.
- Referred clients automatically receive a **10% discount** at checkout.
- Referrers earn a **10% commission** on successful conversions.
- Commission payouts are tracked, with in-app notifications and an admin payout management panel.

### 9. Registration & Onboarding Workflows
- **Self-registration forms** for Clients, Auditors, and Certification Bodies — each with dedicated pages explaining benefits and responsibilities.
- **Shareable invitation links** that admins can send via Email or WhatsApp to onboard new stakeholders.
- **Admin approval workflow** — all new registrations are reviewed and verified before accounts become active.

### 10. Document Management & Audit Trail
- Secure file uploads with per-application document tracking.
- Auditor review status (approved / pending / needs changes).
- Full version history and audit logs for compliance and traceability.

### 11. Notifications & Messaging
- In-app notification center for all users.
- Real-time updates on application status, audit decisions, payouts, and messages.
- Automated notifications when referrals convert and commission becomes payable.

### 12. Admin Control Center
Administrators have access to:

- **User Management** — create, edit, and delete platform users across all roles.
- **Application Oversight** — view, filter, and manage every application in the system.
- **Company Management** — maintain a directory of all client organizations.
- **Auditor Directory** — track auditor availability and assignments.
- **Certification Body Registry** — manage the global CB database with full CRUD.
- **Accreditation Body Registry** — maintain the list of recognized Accreditation Bodies.
- **Regional Admin Management** — assign regional oversight.
- **Registration Requests** — approve or reject new stakeholder signups.
- **Shareable Links Panel** — generate invitation links for any stakeholder type.
- **Reports Dashboard** — review audit logs and analytical documents.
- **Referral Payouts Management** — track and mark commission payouts.
- **Region Configuration** — activate or deactivate operating regions.
- **Roles & Permissions Matrix** — transparent view of what each role can do.
- **General Settings** — platform-wide branding and notification preferences.

### 13. Secure Authentication
- Email/password authentication with strong validation.
- Google OAuth sign-in and sign-up support.
- Password reset flow via secure email links.
- Two-factor authentication option for enhanced account security.

### 14. Modern, Responsive Interface
- Clean, professional dashboard design that works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- Dark-themed interface with a green accent color for a modern, premium feel.
- Fast page loads powered by a modern web stack.
- Fully accessible components with keyboard navigation and screen reader support.

## Recent Major Upgrade — Accreditation & Certification Body Integration

The platform was recently enhanced with a complete overhaul of how Accreditation Bodies and Certification Bodies are managed:

- **New Accreditation Bodies Management Section** — allows Super Admins to maintain the full list of recognized Accreditation Bodies. Regional Admins can contribute new entries.
- **New Certification Body Registry** — a dedicated workspace to register Certification Bodies with full details including linked Accreditation Bodies, countries covered, ISO types, IAF sector codes, and three-tier pricing (initial, surveillance, recertification).
- **Multi-Selection Forms** — each CB can now be linked to multiple Accreditation Bodies, multiple countries (from the full IAF list), multiple ISO standards, and multiple IAF sector codes through easy, searchable dropdowns.
- **Smarter Application Assignment** — admins assigning a CB to an application now choose from the official CB Registry.
- **Payment-Gated CB Visibility** — clients only see their assigned Certification Body (with all its accreditations, country, and website) after completing payment.
- **Full Multi-Language Coverage** — all new features are available in English, Spanish, and Arabic.
